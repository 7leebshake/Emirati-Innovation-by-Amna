About

Engineering with curiosity, ambition, and a bias toward building.

This site is a living archive of projects in engineering, design, fabrication, and research. It exists to document how ideas evolve — from rough direction and constraint mapping to prototype decisions and technical reflection.

The goal is simple: build serious things, document them properly, and keep pushing beyond shallow solutions.

Main themes Thermodynamics, aerospace-driven design, prototyping, CAD, systems thinking.
Best use of this site Project archive, research journal, portfolio, and proof of engineering process.
